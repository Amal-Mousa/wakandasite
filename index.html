About Us

We started our journey April 2019, Wakanda has become a leading multi- disciplined ‘boutique’ recruitment consultancy, holding a workforce of 40 staff across 2 offices that still allows us to maintain that ‘all important’ personal touch. Our main priority at Thor Construct is to satisfy our clients. For this reason, we have built incredible connections in the temporary structure. Our trusted partners will assist you in this field. In addition, our specialists will help you in a vast range of tasks ranging from painting to plumbing, plastering to welding and carpentering to tiling...Etc. On top of that, the motivation levels of our teams and their outstanding personalized services will leave no stone unturned in carrying out your project successfully. We believe relationships are built through transparency and communication; we take time to understand the profile and culture of our clients business as well as the career aspirations of our candidates. We understand it’s a people business...
